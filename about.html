Our Story

Jovy Gadget Store was established in September 2025 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality repair services and products that keep our customers' devices running like new.

Located in Seguku alongside Entebbe Road, we've quickly become the go-to destination for phone repairs, accessories, and new devices in the community. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of the most trusted gadget stores in the area.

Our team of certified technicians has years of experience working with all major phone brands, from Apple and Samsung to Tecno and Infinix. We stay updated with the latest technology and repair techniques to provide the best possible service to our customers.
